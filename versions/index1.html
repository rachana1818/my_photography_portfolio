About Me

The person
behind the lens

Hi! I'm Rachana K, a freelance photographer based in Dallas, Texas. What started as a personal journey — photographing my son's every milestone and small everyday moment — turned into a deep passion for capturing warmth, connection, and quiet joy.

I believe the best photos happen when people feel at ease. My approach is relaxed, personal, and unhurried. I shoot with a prime portrait lens for that dreamy depth, and a kit lens for wider lifestyle shots.
